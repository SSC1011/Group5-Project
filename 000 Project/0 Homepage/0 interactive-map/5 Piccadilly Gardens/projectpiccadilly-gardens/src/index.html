Great music venues in Piccadily Gardens

Piccailly Gardens is situated in the middle of the city centre. It is an open space area which provides both leisure and work oppurtunites for a large number of people.
Picaddilly gardens is surrounded by a unique mix of historic and modern buildings.


The Royal Exchange Theatre

At the Royal Exchange Theatre we tell and share amazing stories both on and off the stage.
Our iconic, spaceship-like theatre-in-the-round can be found in the centre of Manchester, a vibrant and exciting global city with a million stories to share.

Address: St Ann's Square, Manchester M2 7DH


SOUP

A gig & club space in the heart of Manchester’s northern quarter programming forward thinking music and arts events, pulling deeply from our local area as well as artists from further afield.
We are accredited by Attitude is Everything, a charity that works to facilitate better live music spaces for deaf people and people with disabilities. Our staff are trained to meet the needs of all of our customers and we are working constantly to improve our venue’s accessibility.
SOUP Website
Address: 31-33 Spear St, Manchester M1 1DF
